# About me

Hi, I'm Susanna Ahlberg.

I am a former software developer with a passion for writing and communication. My background in software development helps me understand technical systems, while my interest in language, structure and learning helps me explain complex information clearly.

I am especially interested in technical communication that bridges the gap between technical and non-technical audiences. I enjoy creating documentation, guides and learning material that make technical topics easier to understand and use.

## Creative background

Creativity is an important part of how I work. Outside of technical communication, I paint, sing and run a small creative business focused on artistic work.

I am also writing and illustrating a children's book, which has strengthened my interest in storytelling, visual communication and adapting language to a specific audience.

This creative background influences my approach to technical communication. I care not only about accuracy, but also about structure, readability, tone and making information feel accessible to the reader.
