# How to Digitize Traditional Artwork for High-Quality Prints
[Home](../) | [About me](../about.md) | [Selected work](../#selected-work)

This project is a step-by-step guide for artists who want to prepare traditional artwork for high-quality digital prints.

The guide explains the process from photographing or scanning artwork to editing, color correction, file preparation and print-ready export.

## Audience

The guide is written for artists, illustrators and creatives who may not have a technical background but want to understand how to digitize physical artwork in a practical and reliable way.

## Purpose

The purpose of the guide is to make a technical creative process easier to understand and follow.

## My role

I planned, structured and wrote the guide in Markdown. I also organized the content into clear steps and added supporting images to make the process easier to follow.
